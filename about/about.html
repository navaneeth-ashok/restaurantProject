About us

Master Baker opened the first Bread Box Cafe in 2007 with the goal of sharing his passion for hand-crafted bread. Today, we have multiple locations offering you the finest selection of artisanal bread across Toronto.

Bread Box Cafe started as a small bakery cafe in midtown Toronto. Our bread is hand-crafted in small batches to ensure the highest quality. The flour we use for our breads is sourced from a local mill who have been in the business for three generations. Also, we source our ingredients from local farms in Ontario. This spring and summer, the all-time-favorite Peach Crumble Mini Loaf is back. The peaches we use come from Lovely Peach Farms in Niagara. We believe in supporting our local community and we hope that you are able to feel our passion for bread with every bite!

Our breads and pastries are baked fresh every morning using the highest quality ingredients

We are open seven days a week
